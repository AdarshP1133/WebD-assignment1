Expectations

Students have high expectations for college, such as:

  • Rude Professors that don't care
  • Supportive Seniors
  • Messy Hostels
  • Making lifelong friends
  • Having a lot of freedom
  • Studying the exam night and getting 9CGPA
  • Having an easy transition to college life
  • Having a relationship

Reality

However, the reality of college can be quite different:

  • Supportive and Strict Professors
  • Seniors and awesome
  • Hostels are good
  • Making true friends take time and effort
  • Freedom: Not for Freshers
  • Achieving a 8 CGPA is difficult with the workload
  • Transitioning to college life can be challenging
  • Girls are rarer than Aliens
